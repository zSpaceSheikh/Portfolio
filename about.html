About Me

Hello! My name is Zayna Sheikh and I am a Game Designer and Science Illustrator based in Boulder, Colorado. I have a Bachelors of Science in Creative Technology and Design, and a Bachelors of Arts in Astronomy from the University of Colorado Boulder. I’m currently working full time for Sunblink Entertainment on Hello Kitty Island Adventure.

In general, my interests lie in designing experimental and novel interactions through my work in games with a focus on how these sorts of experiences can bring people together and subvert expectations.

I’m also passionate about science communication and using graphic design, illustration, and other creative projects to bring to life astronomy topics and aid in deeper understanding.

When I'm not working, you can find me rock climbing around Boulder, stress baking banana bread, or catching up on my book club reading.

For business inquiries, commissions, collaborations, or to simply say hi, please contact me at: zSpaceSheikh@gmail.com
