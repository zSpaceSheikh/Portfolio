About Me

Zayna Sheikh is an Game Designer and Science Illustrator based in Boulder Colorado, with a Bachelors of Science in Creative Technology and Design and a Bachelors of Arts in Astronomy.

Designing experimental and novel experiences though my work in games has been my focus throughout University and I continue to explore how these kinds of games can bring people together and subvert expectations. My other professional passion involves aiding the scientific community as best I can through creative projects and thoughtful development of communication materials using graphic design.

When I'm not working, you can find me road biking around Boulder, stress baking muffins at home or chasing bugs in Animal Crossing.

For business inquiries, commisions, collaborations or to simply say hi, please contact me at: zSpaceSheikh@gmail.com
